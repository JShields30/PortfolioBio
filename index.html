About me

My name is Joe Shields, I’m a Director of Market Research in Deerfield Illinois. I have many years of experience in qualitative research which includes creating database content, surveys, and managing focus group studies. I also manage my company’s social media sites and website. I have had an interest in computers and technology since I was a child, I would work on computers and other devices either fixing issues or modifying internal components. I always knew I wanted my job to involve technology, since it has always been a big part of my life. I love keeping up with new technology, and my job gives me insight on what new devices or programs are going to be released in the near future.
